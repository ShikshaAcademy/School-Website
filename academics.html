School Academics & Facilities

Empowering students with top-notch facilities, mentorship, and academic excellence.

Sports Facilities

Our school offers a wide range of sports facilities including a state-of-the-art football field, basketball courts, a swimming pool, and a fully equipped gymnasium. We encourage students to participate in various sports events, helping them develop physically and mentally.

Laboratories

Our school has modern science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, fostering an environment where students can engage in hands-on experimentation. We also offer well-equipped computer labs to ensure students stay ahead in the digital world.

JEE Mentorship Program

Our school provides dedicated JEE mentorship programs for students aiming for top engineering institutes. Experienced mentors, personalized guidance, and intensive training sessions help students excel in competitive exams like JEE, KVPY, and more.

Extracurricular Activities

Along with academics, we emphasize on extracurricular activities such as music, drama, art, and debate clubs. These activities enable students to explore their talents and develop a well-rounded personality.
